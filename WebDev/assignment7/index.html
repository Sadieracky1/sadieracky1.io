Despyify Trains Now!

The modern surveillance state has seeped into every aspect of daily life, and public transit systems are no exception. "Despyify Trains Now!" is a speculative project that explores the omnipresence of surveillance cameras in urban transportation, questioning their necessity and the impact on passenger privacy. Through interactive web design, this project aims to visualize the unseen mechanisms of train surveillance.

By leveraging web technologies, "Despyify Trains Now!" will present users with a layered digital experience that simulates how data is collected and processed. The project critiques the normalization of constant monitoring and encourages discussions on alternatives that prioritize security without compromising individual freedoms.
