About These Models

These schematics were designed using a combination of digital simulation software such as Fritzing and Kicad. I have used these for breadboard prototyping, and manual schematic drawing. Components include resistors, capacitors, 555 timers, microcontrollers, various sensors, and much more. Each model was tested for functionality before being documented.
